# TruthGuard — Product Overview

## What It Is
TruthGuard is a social media fact-checking platform that lets anyone instantly verify whether a claim, post, or piece of content circulating on social media is real, misleading, or misinformation.

## The Problem
Misinformation spreads faster than truth on social media. People encounter viral posts, shocking headlines, and fabricated "facts" constantly — and most share without ever verifying. By the time a correction surfaces, the false narrative has already reached thousands. There is no quick, trustworthy tool that fits into the natural flow of scrolling and sharing.

## The Solution
TruthGuard gives anyone — from a concerned citizen to a journalist — a fast, reliable fact-check for any social media claim. Paste a post, paste a URL, or type out a claim. The platform analyzes it against verified sources and returns a clear verdict (True / Misleading / False / Unverifiable), a confidence score, and cited sources — all in seconds.

## Target Users
- **Primary: MZ generation Korean social media users** — Gen Z and younger Millennials who actively scroll, share, and engage on platforms like Twitter/X, Instagram, and TikTok; they encounter suspicious posts constantly and want a fast, casual way to verify before engaging or reposting
- **Secondary: General social media users** — anyone who wants to verify a claim before sharing
- **Secondary: Journalists and fact-checkers** — professionals who need a rapid first-pass research tool

## Core Features
1. **Paste & Verify** — submit any social media post, URL, or typed claim for analysis
2. **Clear Verdict** — color-coded result: True, Misleading, False, or Unverifiable
3. **Confidence Score** — percentage showing how certain the AI analysis is
4. **Source Citations** — linked sources supporting or refuting every verdict
5. **Verification History** — personal log of all previous fact-checks, searchable and re-shareable
6. **Share Verdict** — share the fact-check result back to social media with one click
7. **Trending Claims** — dashboard of most-checked claims across the community

## Brand & Tone
- **App Name**: TruthGuard
- **Tagline**: Verify Before You Share
- **Tone**: Clear, trustworthy, neutral, empowering — never alarmist, never preachy
- **Feel**: A reliable authority — calm, precise, journalistic

## Design Direction
- Primary: Deep navy (#1E3A8A) — trustworthy, authoritative
- Accent: Sky blue (#0EA5E9) — active, credible, digital-native
- Typography: Plus Jakarta Sans (headings) / Inter (body)

## Strategic Principles
1. **Clarity over complexity** — verdicts must be instantly scannable
2. **Trust through transparency** — always show sources and reasoning, never just a bare answer
3. **Neutral stance** — the platform surfaces evidence, it does not editorialize
4. **Speed matters** — a fact-check that takes minutes won't be used at the moment of sharing

## Positioning
TruthGuard sits between full-scale fact-checking newsrooms (too slow) and generic AI chatbots (too opaque). It is built for the moment of doubt — right before someone shares a post — and is fast enough and simple enough for any social media user to reach for by reflex.

## Verified Operation
A **Fact-Check Completed** — a claim submitted, AI-analyzed, and verdict + source citations returned to the user. This is the primary operation TruthGuard exists to perform.
